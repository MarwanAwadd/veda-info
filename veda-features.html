What Veda Does

The features that run a modern clinic.

Veda is organized around how a clinic actually works — patients first, then the operations and finances that support great care.

For your patients and providers

Clinical Care

The Patient Hub

One unified patient record that follows the person across every clinic — never duplicated, always up to date. Each clinic keeps its own chart, notes, and relationship data on top of a single shared identity.

  • Cross-clinic patient lookup before creating a new record
  • Per-clinic chart numbers and relationship status
  • Module-aware tabs for clinical, billing, inventory, schedule
  • Bulk import templates for migrating existing patients

Dermatology Clinical Workspace

The clinical surface for dermatology providers, built around how they actually work — visual, structured, and safe.

  • Body maps and lesion logging tied to the patient
  • Annotated images with full version history
  • Treatment catalog shared with billing and scheduling
  • Consent capture with an immutable e-consent ledger
  • High-risk medication tracking with refill safety

Consents & Documentation

Every signed consent and clinical note is preserved exactly as it was captured — no silent edits, no missing paper.

  • E-consent capture linked to the encounter
  • Append-only consent ledger you can rely on
  • Structured notes alongside free-text observations
  • Per-patient timeline across every clinic visit

For your operations

Run the Practice

Inventory & Regimens

Live stock per clinic, with the difference between back-of-house supplies, retail products, and prescribed regimens built in.

  • Per-clinic inventory items with cost and quantity
  • Append-only waste and discrepancy logs with offsets
  • Retail tagging for products sold to patients
  • Skincare regimens prescribed and dispensed in-app
  • Demand forecasting from upcoming appointments

Smart Scheduling

A calendar that understands availability, resources, and the full appointment lifecycle — and tells the rest of Veda when something happens.

  • Provider availability with real conflict detection
  • Bookable resources for rooms, chairs, and equipment
  • Full lifecycle: booked → confirmed → in-room → checked-out
  • Treatment encounters with multi-phase support
  • Lifecycle events that trigger billing and inventory

Multi-Clinic & Permissions

Built for clinic groups from day one. Each clinic has its own workspace, while group-wide views and admins still see the whole picture.

  • Clinic-group and per-clinic admin roles
  • Four access levels per module: none, read, write, manage
  • One-click scope selector to switch clinics
  • Permission-aware navigation per user
  • Soft deactivation that preserves staff history

For your financial health

Money, Done Right

Cash Flow & Invoicing

An append-only cash-flow ledger and multi-rate invoicing built for the realities of specialty clinics — medical and cosmetic, side by side.

  • Append-only ledger corrected with balancing entries
  • Multi-rate invoicing on a single patient bill
  • Payments, reconciliation, and insurance receivables
  • Treatment-plan financing with tracked progress

Payroll & Commissions

Provider payouts, commissions, and basic payroll grounded in the work that actually happened — not loose spreadsheets.

  • Per-staff records connected to clinics and providers
  • Commission rules tied to performed treatments
  • Basic payroll runs with clear, auditable history
  • Treatment-encounter level cost-of-goods attribution

Revenue Dashboards

Know where the money is coming from and where it is going — today, this month, and across every treatment plan.

  • Actual revenue per treatment plan
  • Outstanding balances and overdue receivables
  • Projected revenue from booked appointments
  • Per-clinic breakdowns with group-wide rollups

For your peace of mind

Trust & Safety

Audit-Ready by Design

Patient PII, cash, waste, and consents are protected with the same level of care you'd expect from your most trusted financial systems.

  • Append-only ledgers for cash, waste, and consents
  • Structured logs with patient-data redaction
  • Per-user, per-clinic, per-entity audit views
  • Soft deactivation so history is never erased

Automated Workflows

The modules talk to each other so your team doesn't have to hand-carry information between systems.

  • Inventory forecasts from upcoming appointments
  • Auto-triggered invoicing on check-out
  • Cost-of-goods attributed to treatments performed
  • Projected revenue surfaced alongside actuals
